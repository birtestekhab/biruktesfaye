About Me

I am a civil and computer science professional with over two decades of experience in highway engineering, construction management, and building projects. My work spans detailed road design, site supervision, project coordination, and the management of complex residential, commercial, and public infrastructure developments.

I have led teams, prepared technical documents, overseen major design and construction workflows, and delivered projects ranging from multi-storey buildings to large-scale road networks across Ethiopia. My education includes a B.Sc. in Civil Engineering and another B.Sc. in Computer Science from Addis Ababa University, complemented by certifications in asphalt works, CAD software, and project management tools.
